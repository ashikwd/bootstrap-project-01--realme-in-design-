About Us

Realm of Design has been designing and creating exquisite architectural details for over 20 years. We bring forth the best in fireplace surround and mantel designs and other architectural concepts for commercial and residential projects in Las Vegas, Los Angeles and across the nation. Our specialty is in artistic, custom, hand-crafted Fireplace Surrounds, Fireplace Mantels, Kitchen hoods, Counter tops, Columns, Faux Wood Beams, Balusters, Fountains and more.

Our pieces can transform your space into a Million Dollar Room. Our custom line of Moldings, trim, shutters and pavers are sure to create a Front Entry that will let your home stand out the way others only dream about. We also carry museum quality one-of-a-kind pieces that are considered works of art. We truly can create or re-create anything.
